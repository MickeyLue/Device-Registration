Connecting Your Device to Encrypted IoT Service

  1. Open the Network Settings on your device and view the list of available Wi-Fi networks.
    • This should be near to where your wireless MAC address was listed.
  2. From the list of available networks, select UofM-IoT and enter the generated password.
  3. Your device is now connected.

Please contact UMN Technology Help if you have problems following the above steps.
